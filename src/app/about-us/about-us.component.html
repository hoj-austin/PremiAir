MEET OUR TEAM

PremiAir Helicopters is a small family of passionate and experienced aviators, adding a personal touch to every flight. Our mission is to share our phenominal view of the world with everyone we can - we can't wait to have you aboard!

JOHN Chief Pilot and Instructor

John began his flying career in the 1980s, having attained both his fixed and rotary wing licenses. His 30+ year long career has spanned mustering, mining, agrigultural flying, the highest level of instructing, powerline work, and media - including piloting the ABC news chopper! Having also gained his Air Transport Pilot License, these days John is focused on sharing his love of flying with his team and his customers.

PAT Professional Pilot

Pat is a passionate and very experienced scenic pilot. Having completed his training here in Melbourne, he's flown several seasons in many different helicopters at some of Australia's most iconic locations - including Ayers Rock and Kings Canyon, as well as Katherine Gorge. Now back in his home town, Pat loves to take people up to see Melbourne the best way - from the air.

HENRY Professional Pilot

Henry obtained his commercial pilots licence right here in Melbourne, at Moorabbin Airport. Throughout his flying career he has flown Robinson R22 and R44 helicopters, as well as AS350 Squirrels, and has been flying out of Essendon for over a year. He is also a qualified Mechanical Engineer - helicopters allow him to combine his passion for flight, and machines!

ROBINSON R44

The Robinson R44 has been the world's best selling helicopter for more than 20 years. A light helicopter with 4 seats, a 6 cylinder piston engine and hydraulic controls, the R44 is exceptionally popular for intimate scenic flights, or a speedy and comfortable cruise to your destination.

AS350 SQUIRREL

The Eurocopter AS350 Squirrel is one of the most popular and versatile work horses of the aviation world. A turbine-engined light and powerful helicopter, it can be both a comfortable, smooth scenic flyer with excellent visibility for all passengers - or a utility machine for fire fighting, powerline or even military operations.

BELL JETRANGER

The Bell 206 JetRanger was originally designed as a light observation helicopter for the US Army, but has since become a family of single and twin turbine engine helicopters used for many civilian and military applications. Like the Squirrel, it is a wonderful host for passengers to to experience the world from the air.
