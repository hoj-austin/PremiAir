DE BORTOLI WINES ESTATE Yarra Valley

First planted in 1971, the vineyard is now planted to 170 Hectares of predominantly Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, Syrah and Cabernet Sauvignon. Recent new vineyards of Pinot Gris, Pinot Blanc and Gamay Noir have been established and are looking quite spectacular. The soils are sedimentary in origin and are known as Humevale Siltstones. The wines produced from these soils are quite aromatic and have very good depth of colour and flavour.

LEVANTINE HILL ESTATE Yarra Valley

Most wineries have a restaurant. Some of them are good. Levantine Hill is exceptional. Just as they seek perfection in their wines, they also want their guests to experience perfection in their restaurants. Which is why they persuaded Teage Ezard to join. With two Chef’s Hats to his name, Teage is one of Australia’s most celebrated chefs. He doesn’t believe in compromise. Only the best will do. And he delivers. Guests at either of the two unique dining spaces will enjoy a truly remarkable gastronomic experience – one where the food has been meticulously crafted to be in perfect harmony with their wines.
