LUXURY FOOD AND WINE PACKAGES

Cruise in a helicopter to the cool climate region of the Yarra Valley, where you can indulge in beautiful cuisine, matched wines and stunning views - before your relaxing return flight to Melbourne. All flights include safety briefings, commentary from your pilot, and phenominal photo opportunities!

DE BORTOLI Yarra Valley

Wine making in Australia since 1928, the De Bortoli family has 90 years of experience and love in every drop of their award-winning wines. With a philosophy that celebrates the region, combined with a passion for sustainable practices, De Bortoli wines are a compliment to the beautiful Yarra Valley landscape. Enjoy a mouth watering celebration of regional produce at their restaurant Locale, an expression of the De Bortoli Italian heritage.

LEVANTINE HILL Yarra Valley

Levantine Hill is a stunning merge of two of the Yarra Valleys best vineyards. In a unique environment of slopes, the natural amphitheatre of Levantine Hill not only results in premium varietals rich in character, but the distinctive terrain makes for a phenomenal views of the vines and estate. Along with an unforgettable wine tasting experience, you can enjoy culinary delights from within their remarkable avant-garde architectural space.
